What InteroHR does

InteroHR is designed to support structured heart rate awareness practice through guided estimation, recovery-focused exercises, and simple performance insights over time.

Pulse awareness training

Practice estimating your own heart rate and compare your estimate with sensor-based measurements.

Recovery-focused sessions

Track recovery patterns after effort and build consistency through repeatable short exercises.

Simple performance insights

Review trends over time to better understand accuracy, consistency, and session-to-session changes.

Grounded in interoception research

InteroHR is inspired by scientific work on interoception — the brain’s ability to sense signals from inside the body, such as heart rate, breathing, and internal arousal.

The app is not a medical tool. It is a structured way to explore heart-rate perception, calibration, and regulation through repeated practice.
