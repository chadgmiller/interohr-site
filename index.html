What makes InteroHR different

InteroHR is not just another app that displays biometrics. It is designed to make interoception trainable by turning internal awareness into a repeatable practice.

Pulse Check

Estimate before reveal

Before seeing your actual heart rate, you pause and sense what your body is telling you. That comparison between estimate and reality is the core training moment.

Recovery Challenge

Practice regulation

Learn how effectively you can downshift after activation. Recovery work turns awareness into action and helps you notice how your body responds over time.

Insights

See patterns, not just sessions

Track whether you tend to underestimate, how consistent you are, and which contexts help or weaken your awareness.

What you are training

Interoception is your ability to sense signals from within your body. InteroHR focuses on heart-rate awareness because it is concrete, measurable, and useful for repeated practice.

  • Calibration: how close your estimate is to reality
  • Bias: whether you tend to overestimate or underestimate
  • Consistency: whether your awareness holds up over repeated sessions
  • Context coverage: whether your skill transfers across rest, stress, caffeine, recovery, and more

Why it matters

Better body awareness can support self-awareness, emotional understanding, and more intentional regulation. The goal is not perfect numbers. The goal is to build a clearer felt sense of what is happening inside you.

InteroHR turns that idea into a simple training loop: estimate, observe, repeat, and learn from your patterns over time.
