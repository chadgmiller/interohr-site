The science behind InteroHR

Why interoception matters

Interoception is your ability to sense what is happening inside your body. It includes signals like heart rate, breathing, tension, and internal activation.

InteroHR focuses on heart-rate awareness because it is concrete, trainable, and easy to revisit over time. Instead of overwhelming this page, the deeper science is now simplified into the essentials.

The short version

Better interoception can support self-awareness, emotional understanding, and regulation. InteroHR turns that idea into practice by helping you estimate, observe, and improve how well you sense your own internal state.

Awareness

Sensing internal signals

Interoception is about noticing what your body is doing, not just reacting after the fact.

Calibration

Comparing felt vs actual

When you estimate heart rate before seeing it, you are training the link between sensation and reality.

Regulation

Learning to downshift

Recovery sessions help you see whether you can settle your system more intentionally over time.

How InteroHR approaches it

The app does not try to explain everything at once. It gives you small repeatable practices, then turns the results into patterns you can understand.

Core training loop

  • Estimate your heart rate before revealing it.
  • Review how close you were and whether you tend to underestimate or overestimate.
  • Repeat across different contexts to build stronger coverage.
  • Use recovery challenges to work on regulation, not just awareness.

What the app tracks

  • Accuracy and overall calibration
  • Bias direction over time
  • Consistency across repeated sessions
  • Coverage across contexts like resting, caffeine, and recovery work

What it looks like in practice

The experience is intentionally clean and lightweight so the focus stays on the signal, not on clutter.

Why this matters

Training interoception is not about chasing perfect numbers. It is about building a more accurate, more reliable felt sense of what is happening inside you, then learning how that awareness changes across context and recovery.
InteroHR is intended for training and self-awareness purposes only and is not medical advice.
